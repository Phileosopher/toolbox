## specific social media - Twitter_X tools

Twitter location-based search:
Insert in search box: geocode:[coordinates],[radius-km], for example: geocode:36.222285,43.998233,2km (only works with km, so 500m = 0.5km). Be aware that it's easy to fake a geolocation for a tweet. - free
