## logistics - aviation - air traffic control tools

[Live Air Traffic Control](https://www.liveatc.net/)
Audio from air traffic control towers in the United States. Aircraft have to identify themselves to ATC towers, so in cases where aircraft are trying to obscure their information from other sites, it might be another way to grab tail numbers or just generally track flights. More complicated to use than e.g. Flightradar24. - free
